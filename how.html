Software Key Feature

1. The system smartly applies conveyor belt & vending machines to replace the conventional medicine collection process.

2. The kiosk can efficiently manage traditional medication dispensing and payment, reducing the need for manual intervention.

System Workflow

  1. When the patient approaches the machine, a standby screen appears. The patient can choose their preferred language.
  2. The patient scans the QR code provided by the doctor. The system will verify the prescription details.
  3. The system prompts the patient to confirm their personal information.
  4. Once confirmed, the machine will begin dispensing the specified medication.
  5. After dispensing, the system returns to the standby screen.
